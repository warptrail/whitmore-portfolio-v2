Hi, I'm Ryan

I’m a web developer and designer seeking to make the Internet a more beautiful and prosperous place. Let us all become connected through this weird and wonderful web to accelerate our information exchange. It will lead to a wiser world.

Web developers of this age are still in the process of laying the tracks for the cyber-bullet trains of the future. We have only scratched the future of what computers and software are capable of. The digital revolution will continue.

About me

I hail from Oakland, California and studied geography at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I have always loved maps and consider myself an explorer of the cyber realm. Web development and cartography are actually somewhat similar in the sense the characteristics of a well built website are like a well drawn map.

When I’m not coding I engage in various artistic activities. Music, graphic design, painting help exercise the other side of my brain. I love hiking and doing yoga to keep up the good fight against gravity.
